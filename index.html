Saudi ecosystem

Saudi Arabia's Vision 2030 and National Transport and Logistics Strategy are reshaping the Kingdom's logistics — through infrastructure investment, customs modernization, port and airport development, special economic zones and the growth of national champions.

ShahnMove is built for organizations operating across this ecosystem and working in coordination with stakeholders such as customs authorities, transport regulators, port and aviation authorities, investment bodies and government-backed infrastructure programs.

Security

Security for sensitive logistics data

ShahnMove is designed for companies and government-backed organizations that work with sensitive logistics, infrastructure and operational data.

The platform helps improve visibility while keeping data access, ownership and security boundaries under control.

  • Role-based access for teams, departments and partners
  • Customer-controlled data ownership
  • Data hosted inside Saudi Arabia, or within your own infrastructure
  • NDA or a full security agreement before any data is shared
  • Secure integrations with existing systems
  • Audit logs for access and data changes
  • Controlled AI analytics within approved data scopes

How it works

From logistics records to decision intelligence

ShahnMove combines a low-lift document workflow with a system-level intelligence layer. It can start with shipment records and expand into infrastructure, operations and planning data without replacing your ERP, WMS or TMS.

01

Share and map

Provide shipment documents, contracts and operational records, then map warehouses, hubs, routes, logistics zones, ports, airports, contractors and dependencies.

02

Extract and structure

Read values, weights, HS codes, charges, parties, dates and operational signals, then structure them into one consistent logistics data layer.

03

Compare and reveal

Identify document discrepancies, compliance gaps, capacity gaps, underused assets, recurring delays, duplication and weak service levels.

04

Guide the roadmap

Deliver an executive view with risks, exposure, bottlenecks, improvement priorities and a practical transformation roadmap.
